Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

Do I need to provide any tools or equipment?

No, our mechanics come fully equipped with all the necessary tools and diagnostic equipment to perform the service at your location. We bring everything needed to complete the job professionally.

How long does a typical doorstep service take?

Service duration depends on the type of service booked. A basic oil change typically takes about 45-60 minutes, while a full service may take 2-3 hours. You'll receive an estimated completion time when booking.

Is the service available in my location?

We currently serve all major cities and many suburban areas across India. During the booking process, you can enter your location to check availability. We're constantly expanding our service areas.

What if additional repairs are needed during service?

Our mechanics will inform you of any additional issues found during inspection. You'll receive a detailed estimate and can approve any extra work before we proceed. No work is done without your consent.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI payments, net banking, and digital wallets. Payment is collected after service completion when you're satisfied with the work done.
